Frequently Asked Questions

What is my account?

When you register on Handicraft.com, your account will be created where you fill in your personal and professional information such as your email id, phone number. and some general information about the craft you deal in.

What is my shop and how do I operate it?

After filling-in your shop information and policies, which include shipment and return policies, your shop will be approved by administrators. Now you can add items by uploading pictures and filling in the relevant product details. You can add/remove products when and as you want in your shop.

How do I know my order has been confirmed?

Once you've successfully placed an order on Handicraft.com, you will receive a confirmation email containing all the details of your purchase.

Is it possible to order bespoke/made-to-order and “out-of-stock” products?

Yes, Handicraft.com offers the option to order bespoke or made-to-order products. Additionally, you can inquire about out-of-stock items and our team will assist you with the availability and order process.

Is it necessary to have a registered account on Handicraft.com to shop any product?

While having a registered account offers convenience, it is not mandatory. You can browse and shop on Handicraft.com as a guest user.

How do I make payment for a purchase from Handicraft.com?

Handicraft.com accepts various payment methods including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and online payment gateways. Simply choose your preferred payment option during checkout.

Can the products be shipped internationally?

Yes, Handicraft.com ships products internationally. You can select your desired shipping destination during the checkout process.

How do I get more marketing support from Handicraft.com?

For marketing support and assistance with promoting your products on Handicraft.com, please reach out to our dedicated support team. We offer various marketing solutions to help showcase your products to a wider audience.

Do you have any listing charges?

At Handicraft.com, we do not have any listing charges. You can list your products on our platform without incurring any additional fees.
